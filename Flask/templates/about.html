About Smart Lender

An end-to-end machine learning web application that predicts whether a loan applicant should be Approved or Rejected, helping financial institutions make faster and more consistent lending decisions.

How It Works

1 Applicant submits details through the prediction form
2 Input is preprocessed identically to the training pipeline
3 Ensemble model predicts outcome + approval probability
4 Confidence score + recommendation displayed with decision
