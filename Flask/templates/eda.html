Key EDA Insights

  • Dataset: 614 loan applications
  • Features: 12 (mix of numeric & categorical)
  • Target imbalance: ~69% Approved, ~31% Rejected
  • Strongest predictor: Credit_History
  • Income is right-skewed; most applicants earn < ₹10K
  • Semiurban properties have highest approval rate
